Introduction

English for Kids is a webpage to help children learn English. We have a lot of new words in some topic such as Family, Flowers, Universe, Fruits, Animals and so on. In addition, we have some game to help children relax and review vocabulary. Finally, our web have Short Story topic that help children improve their listening skill.



Guide

At homepage, you click on 'Start here' or select on menu bar.

Vocabulary view, you can choose topic's vocabulary and learn new words with photos and sounds.

Game view, there are some game in there. You can choose game that you like it and play it.

Short Story view, it is similar with Game view so that you can choose story to listen or read it.
